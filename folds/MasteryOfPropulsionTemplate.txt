Header: Subject,Date,Trial,File,0,30,60,90,120,150,180,210,240,270,FinalScore

Subject is the subject's number (e.g., 14)
Date is the date of the trial in MM/DD format
Trial is the trial number recorded
File is the kinematic mat file that stores the kinematic data for the trial
    It is important that the entry is only written down if the kinematic data is 'ok' or better
0,30,60,90,120,150,210,240,270 are the interval scores for the respective 30 second interval
Final Score is the written down final score used to verify everything was written down correctly



Example:
Subject,Date,Trial,File,0,30,60,90,120,150,180,210,240,270,FinalScore
